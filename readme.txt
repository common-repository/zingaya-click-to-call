Let your website visitors call you. Without a phone. Zingaya enables voice calls through any computer, right from a webpage.

== Description ==
Zingaya enables voice calls through any computer, right from a webpage. No download or phone is required. Zingaya offers this seamless voice calling capability to website operators – whether it’s a huge e-commerce enterprise or a startup company. Simply embed a “Call” button into your website. Visitors can click that button and the call is immediately forwarded to your landline or mobile phone. All you need is a website; all your visitors need is a browser and microphone. It’s that easy.

== Installation ==
= From your WordPress dashboard =
1. Visit `Plugins > Add New`
1. Search for `Zingaya`
1. Activate Zingaya from your Plugins page. (You will be greeted with a Welcome page.)
1. Specify your Zingaya username/password or create new Zingaya account on a Welcome page.
1. Open Widgets section and click Widget is active checkbox to enable widget on your website.
1. You can customize look&feel and position of the widget by using Widget builder link (under widget name).

= From WordPress.org =
1. Download Zingaya.
1. Upload the `zingaya` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
1. Activate Zingaya from your Plugins page. (You will be greeted with a Welcome page.)
1. Specify your Zingaya username/password or create new Zingaya account on a Welcome page.
1. Open Widgets section and click Widget is active checkbox to enable widget on your website.
1. You can customize look&feel and position of the widget by using Widget builder link (under widget name).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= What embedding options are available? =
You can choose from 2 options - widget and button. Zingaya click-to-call button can be embedded anywhere using shortcode, template tag or WP widget. Zingaya click-to-call widget can be located on the left or right part of the page (in the middle) - it will have fixed position (and follow website visitor while scrolling).
